Shipping Policy

Most standard shipments are sent through USPS, UPS ground, or FedEx ground. The exact service depends on the size and weight of your order.

  • Standard
  • Free for guest orders of $150 or more
  • $10 for guest orders of less than $150
  • (5-10 business days for orders placed by 5pm EST)
  • Priority
  • $20 for guest orders
  • (3-5 business days for orders placed by 5pm EST)
  • Express
  • $30 for guest orders
  • (2-3 business days for orders placed by 5pm EST)
  • International
  • $50 for guest orders
  • (10-14 business days for orders placed by 5pm EST)

Additional information

  • We proccess orders Monday-Friday (excluding holidays).
  • If you place your order after the cutoff times, we'll proccess and ship the order the next business day.
  • We do not deliver on Sundays or holidays.
  • We do not offer PO boxers, re-shippers, or package forwarding services.

Easy Returns & Free exchanges

We hope you love your X-Under product. But if not, we accept returns and offer free size exchanges within 30 days from your date of purchase.
