FAQ

Where do you ship?

We're happy to ship your order anywhere in the U.S., internationally.

Help! I Keep missing my delivery. What should I do?

If you are not available to receive your delivery, the carrier will leave a contact card at your address saying when they will attempt to re-delivery the package. If you are not able to receive the parcel by the third delivery attempt, the parcel will be returned to the carrier depot. If you don't collect it or arrange re-delivery, the parcel will be sent back to the A-T-Shirt Return Department. We will proccess your refund within 7 business days after receiving your return. Durning the holiday season (December-February), returns may take up to 14 business days to proccess due to higher than mormal volumes. Refunds will be issued in the original form of payment. It the refund is applied to your credit card, your credit card company may take uo to 10 additional business days to post the refund to your account.

Can I Ship to an Address other than my billing address?

Yes. You'll be given this option during online checkout.
