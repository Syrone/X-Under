6 tips to create a perfect t-shirt design

In the past couple of years, t-shirt design has been taking a new turn toward fashion and excellence. Buying a standard pre-designed t-shirt isn't as interesting as it used to be. After all, meeting someone with the same t-shirt design at work or school is less than exciting. That's why custom design is gaining momentum with the speed of light. With the opportunity to create your own t-shirt readily available, you face a simple question. What should your design be like, anyway? Here are a few tips to push you in the right direction.

  1. Go for a Walk
    • If you are out of ideas, take a walk. You may be surprised at how many ideas surround you when you aren't looking. Ask nature to give you a push forward. It has inspired thousands of artists. Why shouldn't it share a few pointers with you? Take photos and then turn them into designs.
  2. Keep it simple
    • The key purpose of a custom t-shirt design is to share something with others. Be it your inner world or special fashion views, you want them to be obvious. The design should be readable at one glance. If it takes forever to decipher it, no one will try. So keep things simple.
  3. Be Careful about humor
    • If you want your t-shirt to be humorous, make sure to keep it subtle. It's hard to find a balance between subtleness, provocation, and cheap appearance. So if you aren't sure that your t-shirt doesn't offend anyone, consider another design.
  4. Choose proper colors
    • Combining different colors could be the toughest part of the t-shirt design. You need to make sure they match. Otherwise, even the most intricate design will blur into a colorful stain. Take the time to figure out which colors look good against the background you've chosen or vice versa.
  5. Check out the latest trends
    • If you need some inspiration, check out the latest design trends. Just make sure you use them for ideas only. Becoming a copycat isn't just unfair, it's often illegal. Add a personal touch to the design you like and come up with your own masterpiece.
  6. Find the right company
    • If you want your design to appear exactly as you wish, you need to find the right t-shirt printing company. Only the highest quality equipment coupled with the work of professional designers can help you create a t-shirt you've always dreamed about. Designing t-shirts can be a bit harder than you think. If you aren't sure your design works, ask for help from the professionals. You may be surprised at how well they can turn your idea into reality.
