  • Purchase and return information and history, including our store locations you may have purchased from.
  • Account or member program information, including login and password, account settings, and communication preferences in connection with marketing. Account information also includes information you populate in your account, preferences and interests you’ve shared with us, and can include optional demographic information.
  • When you set up an account with us, that information is your unique account across the website and mobile app.

INTEREST-BASED ADVERTISING

We permit our trusted business partners and third-party advertising companies (including ad servers, ad agencies, technology vendors or providers of sponsored content) to collect your browsing information and numerical identifiers associated with your browser or device in order to provide ads on our behalf that are tailored to your interests.

Using tracking technologies, discussed in more detail under “Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies” below, these companies collect data from a particular browser or device about a user’s web viewing behaviors over time, across unrelated websites, and in some cases combine such data with data collected from other browsers or devices associated with the same user or household, and use such data to predict user preferences and to deliver advertisements to that user which are calculated to target that user’s predicted preferences across devices.

Our systems do not recognize browser “Do Not Track” signals, but several of the third-party advertising companies that utilize cookies on our website to provide interest-based advertising enable you to opt out of those practices. If you would like to learn more about interest-based advertising and see what choices you may have about how companies use your information, please go to aboutads.info or networkadvertising.org. We do not operate or control these sites, and are not responsible for the opt-out choices available there. It is important to note that in most cases opting out drops an “opt-out cookie” on your browser. As a result, if you delete cookies or use a different computer or browser, you’ll need to renew your opt-out choice.

Another way to opt-out of or control the collection of data for interest-based advertising is to adjust your browser settings as further described in the “Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies” section below.

Please remember, even if you opt out of interest-based advertising on or through these sites and other opt-out sites or services, or by adjusting your browser settings, you may still receive ads from X-Under LLC., as interest-based ads are only one form of advertising.
